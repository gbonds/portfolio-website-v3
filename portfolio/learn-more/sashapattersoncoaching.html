Sasha Patterson Coaching

Mobile-first informational site for a new service.

  • Web Design
  • Static Site Development
  • Responsive Design

I designed and developed this concept website for a hypothetical client who is starting a new life coaching service. Goals include establishing the client's brand in a personal and unique way, providing information about services, and encouraging customer engagement.

I designed a mobile-first site with just one page so that visitors can seamlessly scroll from one section to the next. I kept the look and feel of the website as simple as possible, while also keeping it warm and friendly. I made use of CSS flexbox to ensure that the design is responsive to any browser size.
